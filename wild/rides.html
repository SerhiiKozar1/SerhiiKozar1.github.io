Vortex

Can't get enough of adrenaline rush and screaming for more, TurboTwister is next up for guests to experience a dark ride through high speed, twists and turns

Kraken Racers

Four-lane KrakenRacer (competitive mat racing slides), FreeFall (high speed body slide) and TurboTwister (high speed enclosed body slide).

Royal Flush

The Signature Ride comprises of the BehemothBowl (all time popular bowl ride), and the newest TornadoWave.

Free Fall

Dare yourself in another adrenaline rushing experience.

Torpedo

Experience free-falling from four storeys high and twist and turn on Torpedo! Who knew G-Force could be this fun!

Slide Up

Imagine plunging down a four-storey ramp, accelerating until you hit the deck and skimming across to soar on the opposite side.

Ular-Lah

The first raft slide in Southeast Asia, Ular-Lah is a super flume ride that offers you a chance to brave the rapids.

The Water Works

Slam your way through wacky curves and crazy turnings with The Waterworks, the high speed flume ride that makes a maze.

Kidz Zone

Introducing the KIDZ Zone, pint-sized versions of the big rides are designed to provide maximum entertainment to the little ones while maintaining maximum peace of mind for the parents too.

Splash Play

Families with young ones can enjoy the all-new Splash Play where little ones can slosh around the low water with fun jets and water sprays.

Professor's Playground

Slide and Swing the Splashy Style! Looking for a place that will generates hours of fun for your kids?

Yippie

Yippie! Is a shallow sloshing area filled with soft play equipment designed to provide a safe and fun environment for toddlers.

Shiok River

Shiok River skirts the perimeter of the park, meandering through a variety of lush landscape that will leave you soothed and invigorated.

Tsunami

Tsunami combines the best of swimming pools and the sea to give you the excitement of the surf under controlled conditions.

Jacuzzi

Fluid jets to rub and ease the kinks in your muscles plus a bubbly massage that soothes your aches.
