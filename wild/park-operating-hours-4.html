Park rules & regulations

For the enjoyment and safety of park guest, the following rules and regulations shall be abided upon entry into Wild Wild Wet:

Guest shall:
  • 1. 0bserve the dress code (modest swimwear or shorts and T-shirts) at all times. Click here for Dress Code Guide.
  • 2. Be responsible for the safety of their children and ensure that their children are under the supervision of an adult at all times.
  • 3. 0bserve all warning and safety instruction signs.
  • 4. Assess their own physical condition and exercise good judgement before using any of the slides and pools.
  • 5. The use of lifejacket is encouraged.
  • 6. Act sensibly and follow the instructions of the lifeguards and staff of Wild Wild Wet.
  • 7. Ensure that their belongings are attended to at all times.
Guest shall NOT:
  • 1. Smoke in the park area, inclusive of the pools, slides and queue lines.
  • 2. Bring pets or animals into Wild Wild Wet. Only guide dogs are permitted into the park.
  • 3. Consume alcohol in slides and pools.
  • 4. Bring or consume food and drinks purchased or prepared outside Wild Wild Wet.
  • 5. Engage in commercial filming or photography without the Management's approval.
  • 6. Tamper with any of the rides, slides, equipment, facilities or fixtures.
